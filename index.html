This is a club whose primary focus is on K-pop. In this club during meetings members will have listening parties for both old and new generations of K-pop, learn choreography from their favourite groups, and also have K-drama watch parties and top of many more activities. This is a club where people can be free to enjoy the music, culture and food from Korea and share that appreciation with other like-minded individuals..
