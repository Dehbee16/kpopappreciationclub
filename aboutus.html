What We Do

This club was founded in September 2025 by a handful of passionate students wh had a strong desire to connect and bonds with others over their shared interest of K-pop. What started as small meeting in the learning commons has ballooned nto a well organized team and club that boasts a comfortable 30 members so far on the Trafalgar campus. Besides our shared love of K-pop we are also dedicated to supporting and donating to good causes and collect weekly dues to support the charities we work with. We are so excited to meet you if you decided to join our diverse family! ;) We meet every Friday at 4pm -5/6pm in room j222
